Our Approach

Purpose-Driven Capital Strategy

We work with you to ensure integrated, holistic solutions so that every element of guidance you receive is aligned with your impact goals — and your resources are managed to care for and support what is essential.

1

Clarify Purpose & Develop Your Impact Strategy

We begin by clarifying or affirming what is most important to you and/or your institution, the change and transformation you seek to advance, the power and influence your capital holds, and the impact commitments that will guide all future decisions. This becomes the foundation for all capital strategy work.

2

Thrive Because You Have Enough

In coordination with aligned financial planners and tax professionals, we help you identify how much is enough to ensure you, your family, or your institution can thrive, clarify which resources must grow and which must sustain, and ensure that stewardship needs are integrated with impact commitments. Purpose and thriving are addressed together — not separately.

3

FS Capital Dashboard

See, design and guide the waterways through which your capital flows, clarifying the role of each pool of capital. Working alongside our aligned Registered Investment Advisor (RIA) partners we design a capital allocation plan aligned with your impact strategy, clarify the role of each pool of capital, and align risk, return, liquidity, and time horizons with purpose.

4

Integrated-Capital IPS (IC-IPS)™

Develop detailed impact plans by pool of capital. For each pool within your allocation plan, we define impact intent and priorities, establish criteria for opportunities and managers, clarify strategic parameters, and ensure coherence across the whole portfolio.

5

Portfolio Strategy & Implementation

We work with you and aligned financial advisory partners and managers for each pool of capital to translate impact plans into portfolio strategies, support decision-making and opportunity evaluation, and ensure implementation remains aligned with purpose.

6

Monitor, Evaluate & Evolve

We support you to establish appropriate impact monitoring practices, create review and reflection cadences, and adapt strategy as learning and conditions evolve.

7

Integrated Advisory

You may already have trusted financial service providers who need support implementing your impact directives, need a full suite of aligned partners to manage significant new resources, or be seeking a new partner for a specific component of your capital strategy. Whatever the case, Full Spectrum Advisors ensures your financial ecosystem operates in alignment — so that all guidance supports your impact goals and protects what is essential to your thriving.
